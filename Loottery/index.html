FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What is Loottery?

Loottery is the first NFT lottery that will give you a chance to win Blue-chips on a monthly basis.

How does it work?

Each month we will sell 12,500 Loottery tickets with a minting price of 0.08E and we will conduct 5 raffles per event. That means your ticket could win up to 5 times.

What about the prizes?

Our prizes will consist of Blue-chips and mainstream NFTs. For example: CryptoPunks, BAYC, AB Archetypes, XCopy Guzzlers, Chromie Squiggles, Cool Cats, Cryptoadz, Mekaverse, Meebits, Loot, Metaheros, 0n1 Force, etc. The total value of these prizes will be around 700 to 750 ether each month.

What about the raffle?

A snapshot will be taken 3 days after the event is SOLD OUT, and the prizes will be allocated via Chainlink VRF.

What happens if it takes too long for a Sold Out?

If it takes more than 2 days to be considered a SOLD OUT, each time 20% of the tickets for a specific event are sold, a snapshot will be taken and the prizes will be allocated via Chainlink VRF

Is this a one-time event?

We will conduct 12 Loottery events in a 12 months period, each consisting of 5 raffles as stated before. That means there will be 12 different loottery tickets at the end of the year. Each event will have a specific name, “GENESIS” being the first one.

What happens with my tickets after the event?

Holders of a full set of tickets (12 different tickets – 1 for each event) will participate in a special raffle with TOP TIER NFT Prizes Only. One entry for each full set owned.
